    Frequently Asked Questioins

    These are the most asked questions.

    • How do I book an appointment?

      To book an appointment you first need to login through your Patient ID and then click on Request an appointment.

    • After I click on Request an appointment is my appointment confirmed?

      NO. After you request an appointment, your request goes to the doctor and if he approves the reqested time and date then you will get the appointment confirmation.

    • How do I know about my doctor?

      We have provided the information about the doctors on the website.

    • Why am I not able to create a new account?

      You are not able to make a new account because you first need to contact the dentist and then the doctor will give you your unique login details.
